Welcome to my personal site.

This space brings together a few different parts of my work and learning. It is
part portfolio, part notebook, and part place to keep track of projects as they
grow.

Here you can expect to find:

- project writeups about software, web development, data, and low-code tools
- short articles and notes from things I am learning or building
- updates as the site itself evolves over time

The site is still taking shape, so some pages may feel lightweight for now. The
goal is to keep improving it gradually and use it as a practical record of what
I am working on.
